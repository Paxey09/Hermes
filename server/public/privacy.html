Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 23, 2026

1. Information We Collect

Hermes is an AI-powered Messenger chatbot that collects the following information:

  • Messages: Text messages you send to the Hermes chatbot on Messenger
  • User ID: Your Facebook Messenger user ID (required to respond to messages)
  • Metadata: Timestamps of your interactions with the bot

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used exclusively to:

  • Process your messages and generate AI-powered responses
  • Improve the chatbot's performance and accuracy
  • Maintain service reliability and security
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Data Storage and Security

We employ industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • Data is stored securely on our servers hosted by Render
  • All communications use HTTPS encryption
  • We do not share your personal data with third parties without consent
  • Access to data is restricted to authorized personnel only

4. Third-Party Services

Hermes uses the following third-party services:

  • Facebook/Meta: Message delivery and user authentication
  • OpenRouter: AI model API for generating responses
  • Render: Server hosting and infrastructure

These services have their own privacy policies. We recommend reviewing them.

5. User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time by blocking or discontinuing use of the chatbot

6. Data Retention

Message data is retained for up to 30 days to improve service quality. After 30 days, messages are automatically deleted unless retention is required for legal or security purposes.

7. Children's Privacy

Hermes is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

10. Compliance

Hermes complies with:

  • GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation)
  • CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act)
  • PH-DPA (Philippine Data Privacy Act)
  • Facebook Platform Policies
